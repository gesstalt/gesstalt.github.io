This project is about developing an end-to-end software framework to create augmented reality based content from scanned or camera captured images.

Design and Development of A Software Platform to Create Immersive Content from Digitized and Born Digital Documents



  1. Funding Agency: iHub Drishti, IIT Jodhpur, DST
  2. Duration: 3 years (2024-2027)

As part of this project, we have published a labelled datasets for this purpose.

IMCDB (Indian Mythological Comic Database)

Description: A dataset of digitized comic storybooks in the English language with ground truth annotations for each panel in pages and ground truth text files for each narration box and speech balloon within a panel. Additionally, ground truth binary masks of speech balloons and narration box for each page.
