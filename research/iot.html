  1. Lungs Compliance Meter using Ultrasonic Sensors [Along with Dr. Sabyasachi Sircar, AIIMS Jodhpur]

    Abstract: Lungs Compliance Meter is a simple automated device to demonstrate the measure of volume/pressure change in human lungs using the compliance curve. It is working model for teaching Dynamic Airway Compression.
